We Need Your Support

LitecoinZ is a project developed and supported entirely by the community. We would need to establish a fund to allow initial adoption but from there we aim to make it a self sustained economy. Become part of it by contributing with your donations.

Why do we need donations?

We need donations to help us pay for services and infrastructures that are necessary for the development and evolution of our cryptocurrency.

How they will be used?

  • Registration fees for the exchange markets
  • Domain name renewals
  • Infrastructure services
  • Wallets
  • Future projects
